Welcome to Fresh Blue Ink.

Our goal is to publish quality Canadian fiction in electronic and traditional formats.

Areas of editorial interest include science fiction, speculative fiction, psychological fiction, and bildungsroman.

Our first project, the novel Breakaway: 1977, was released Fall 2013.

A short story anthology, Space Lizards Of Canada, is open to submissions.
